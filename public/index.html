What is Versus?


A Site To Challenge Your Friends.

Here, you can challenge a friend in a game of currently, only Connect-4. With a simple to use room system, one player can make a room and the other can simply enter the room code to join them. If you have something to prove or just want a mediocre way to show-off your intellectual prowess, Versus is the site to do that!


Still Growing!

While Connect-4 is the only game available currently, the project is still being worked on and there are more games to come. If you have any suggestions to what games to make next, email Jason at: officialjasonkim@gmail.com.


Available Games


Connect-4

Battle a friend in a not-so-fierce game of Connect-4. The rules are the same as the real Connect-4 tabletop game. With gravity not being negligble, you and your opponent take turns dropping colored pieces until on you get 4 in a row. You are the yellow pieces, your opponent is the red pieces, and a reset button is there for you to play again.
